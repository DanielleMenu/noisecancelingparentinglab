The Problem

Preparing for a baby can feel like drinking from a firehose.

Every website has a different checklist. Every influencer has a different recommendation. Every company has a different "must-have."

Somehow you're expected to turn all of that into a shopping list.

```
```

What It Does

Registry Auditor

Review common registry items and understand what is essential, optional, or often unnecessary.

Wait-and-See Framework

Identify purchases that can be delayed until you know your baby's needs and preferences.

Sanity Checks

Practical explanations designed to reduce overwhelm and support confident decisions.

```
```

Designed For

✓ First-time parents

✓ Parents building a baby registry

✓ Families trying to avoid overspending

✓ Anyone feeling overwhelmed by baby gear advice

```
```

What It Isn't

This isn't a minimalist challenge.

This isn't a judgment of how other people parent.

And it definitely isn't another list of things you're supposed to feel guilty about.

The goal is simple:

Help you spend less time researching products and more time preparing for your actual baby.
